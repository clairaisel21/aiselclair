Prayers in a time of coronavirus, Working for your kingdom Lord Jesus, give us the grace to hear your voice, And the strength to continue working for your kingdom in this time of pandemic. Do not allow us to be indifferent to those who today suffer the loss of a loved one, or who suffer from the absence of work. But give us the courage to accompany and side with those who suffer violence or injustice. Amen.
